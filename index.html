About Illinois

Illinois is a Midwestern state. Springfield is the capital, and Chicago is the largest city. The state touches the Mississippi and Ohio Rivers and meets Lake Michigan in the northeast.

Source: Wikipedia — Illinois

Quick Snapshot

  • Region: Midwest, USA

  • Largest city: Chicago
